Task 1

Development

For the development of my character, I made a concept sketch to explore the appearance, proportions, aesthetics and functionality in terms of movment. The next stage of character development was to create the initial shape of the character in blender, and then animate the characters movment.

Testing 1 - head

Test/Input Expectation Output
1. Inspect initial shape and size of head from low poly model.   Make the shape of the head more proportionate to the rest of the body to be more in line with a human body shape and proportions.  

Feedback 1

Q. what do you think needs improving?

"Needs a lot of work but the height of it is good."

Q. what do you think needs improving?

"Make it round, more in line with the shape of a human head."

Improvements 1

"I made the head more round like the shape of a human head, to show the proportions better in relation to a human head."

Testing 2 - arms

Test/Input Expectation Output
1. Inspect initial shape and points for future articulation of the arm from the low poly model - needs to be capable of arm-like movement.   To make it more realistic and more in proportion to a human arm relative to a body, and able to move like a human arm.  

Feedback 2

Q. What needs improving?

"The detail of the arm."

Q. What needs improving?

"The joint needs to also be connected with articulation points to allow for future movement."

Improvements 2

"I fixed the joint so the arm is attached to the body, and made it more detailed so that it functions like a human arm."
